IB Student Profile

The goal of all IB programs is to develop international thinking among people who, recognizing common humanity and shared responsibility for the planet, help create a better and more peaceful world.

Researchers

We develop curiosity, strive for research and knowledge. We are able to learn independently and together with others, we support the love of learning throughout our lives.

Knowledgeable

We study conceptual understanding, explore knowledge covering a wide range of disciplines. We address issues and ideas of local and global importance.

Thinkers

We use critical and creative thinking skills to analyze and take responsibility for complex problems.

Caring

We show empathy, compassion and respect. We strive to serve society by making the world a better place.
